Privacy Policy — Tower Finder

Effective date: 28 April 2026 · Last updated: 28 April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information the Tower Finder mobile application ("the App", "we", "us") collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. Tower Finder is published by AHZ Global Services.

1. Information we collect

1.1 Account information (provided by you)

  • Email address
  • Password (stored hashed by Firebase Authentication; we never see your password)
  • Optional profile fields you fill in: full name, phone number, company, role/title, years of experience, location (text), bio, skills, availability flag

1.2 Information collected automatically

  • Device location: precise GPS location, used only when the App is open and only to show towers near you, plan routes, and label trip receipts. Location is not transmitted to our servers; it is used on your device. The most recent location label (e.g. "Karachi, Pakistan") is cached on your device for one hour to reduce GPS use.
  • Cellular network info: the cell ID and signal strength of the tower currently serving your phone, used only to display the "Serving Tower" card on the home screen. Stored on your device only.
  • Crash reports: if the App crashes, an automatic anonymous crash report is sent to Firebase Crashlytics so we can fix bugs. These reports do not contain your name, email, location, or any user-entered content.

1.3 Trip-quotation activity log

When you generate and share a trip quotation from the Route screen, the App silently records the following operational data to a private Google Sheet:

  • Quote ID (a random number)
  • Your display name and email (already in your account)
  • Date the quote was generated
  • Number of sites in the route, total distance, total duration, fuel estimate
  • List of public site IDs visited (e.g. "JZ-HYD-001")
  • The Google Maps URL for the route

This log helps us understand how the route feature is used so we can improve it. We do not log your live GPS coordinates, phone number, address, or any other private details.

2. How we use your information

  • To let you sign in to the App and remember you between sessions
  • To show towers near your location, plan routes, and label receipts
  • To respond to messages you send via the in-app Contact form
  • To show non-personalised banner advertisements (see "Advertising" below)
  • To diagnose crashes and improve App stability

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Telecom site data

The App uses a database of public telecom cell-site information for Pakistan, sourced from publicly available references. This data describes cell-tower locations operated by Jazz, Telenor, Ufone, and Zong. The App does not collect or transmit any telecom-operator confidential data.

4. Advertising

Tower Finder shows banner advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect a limited set of identifiers (such as the Google Advertising ID) to serve and measure ads. You can reset or limit ad personalisation at any time from your phone's Settings → Google → Ads.

Read Google's privacy policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy. Tower Finder is configured to request non-personalised ads by default in regions that require it.

5. Third-party services we use

  • Firebase Authentication and Firestore (Google) — to manage your account and store your profile fields
  • Firebase Crashlytics (Google) — to receive anonymous crash reports
  • Google Maps — to open the optimised route in your phone's Google Maps app
  • Google Forms — to receive contact-form messages and trip-quotation logs
  • TinyURL — to shorten Google Maps share links
  • AdMob — to display banner ads

6. Permissions the App requests

  • Location (Fine and Coarse) — to show nearby towers and plan routes from your position. The App still works without this; you just won't see distance-based features.
  • Read Phone State — to read which cell tower is currently serving your phone. The App still works without this; the Serving Tower card just won't populate.
  • Storage / Photos — only to save your trip-quotation receipt images to your phone's gallery. The App does not read your other photos.
  • Notifications (Android 13+) — reserved for future updates such as a database-refresh banner. We do not send marketing or promotional notifications.
  • Internet — to download the latest tower database, sign in, send contact messages, and load ads.

7. Your choices and rights

  • You can stop using the App at any time and uninstall it.
  • You can request deletion of your account and associated profile data by contacting us at the email below. We delete account data within 30 days of a confirmed request.
  • You can deny or revoke any individual permission from your phone Settings → Apps → Tower Finder → Permissions.
  • You can opt out of personalised advertising as described in section 4.

8. Data retention

  • Account data (email, profile) is kept until you request deletion.
  • Trip-quotation logs are kept for as long as the linked Google Sheet retains them (no automatic expiry).
  • Cached location and serving-cell data live only on your device and are cleared when you uninstall the App.

9. Children's privacy

Tower Finder is intended for telecom professionals and the general adult public. It is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has created an account, contact us and we will delete the account.

10. Security

We use industry-standard practices including HTTPS for all network requests and Firebase's managed authentication. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security but commit to addressing any breach promptly.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Significant changes will be flagged inside the App. Continued use of the App after an update constitutes acceptance.
